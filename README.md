A civic-awareness digital platform focused on transparency, accountability, and responsible reporting of local issues in **Muktainagar, Maharashtra**.

---

## 📌 About the Project

**Silent Nyay Muktainagar** is a community-focused initiative designed to bring attention to civic issues such as:

- Land disputes & encroachments  
- Governance inefficiencies  
- Public infrastructure concerns  
- Citizen rights & accountability  

The platform promotes **fact-based awareness**, encouraging lawful resolution while maintaining a **neutral and non-personal approach**.

---

## 🎯 Vision & Mission

### Vision
To create a transparent and informed local ecosystem where citizens are aware, responsible, and empowered.

### Mission
- Encourage **lawful awareness over conflict**
- Promote **transparency in local governance**
- Enable **responsible digital reporting**
- Support **community-driven accountability**

## 🚀 Core Features

- 📢 Issue-based awareness content  
- 🏡 Focus on land & legal concerns
